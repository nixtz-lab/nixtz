Featured Lesson: How to Find Your "Number"

This calculator uses the **4% Rule** to find your "Target Nest Egg". This is a common guideline for financial independence.

  • The 4% Rule: A guideline stating you can safely withdraw 4% of your invested nest egg each year without running out of money.
  • How We Calculate It: We take your `(Desired Monthly Income * 12)` and divide it by `0.04` (or multiply by 25) to find your target.
  • Estimated Annual Return: We suggest 7-8% to be conservative. The S&P 500 has historically averaged ~10%, but 7% accounts for inflation.
