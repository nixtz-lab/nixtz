Crypto Strategies

Applying the "Think Money Tree" philosophy to digital assets. Learn to research, plan, and automate your crypto investing.

See Crypto Market Crypto for Beginners

Three Core Approaches to Crypto

1. Fundamental Research (The TMT Way)

A strategy is useless if the asset is worthless. Before investing, you *must* apply value principles.

  • Research the Team & Backers.
  • Analyze the Whitepaper & Roadmap.
  • Verify the Project's Utility & User Base.
  • Understand the Tokenomics (Supply).

2. Automated Trading (Bots)

Use bots to execute a strategy 24/7. This is *active trading*, not passive income.

  • Grid Bots (Spot/Infinity) profit from volatility in a set range.
  • AI-Powered Bots use algorithms to find trading opportunities.
  • Warning: A bot only automates your plan. A bad plan will just lose money faster.
  • Exchanges like Pionex, KuCoin, & Bybit offer these.

3. Passive Accumulation & Earning

The true "Money Tree" methods. Set it, forget it, and let it grow.

  • DCA Bots (Cost Averaging) automate buying a fixed amount over time.
  • Earn Interest on Stablecoins: Lend your USDT on platforms like Crypto.com for yields, often between 2% - 7% APY.
  • Staking: Lock up proof-of-stake coins (like ETH) to help secure the network and earn rewards.

Featured Lesson: How to Research a Crypto Project

Don't gamble. Invest. Before you deploy a single bot or strategy, you must research the asset. This is the TMT philosophy.

  • Who is the team? Are they public and experienced?
  • What is the roadmap? Have they hit past milestones?
  • Is there a user base? Do people actually use this?
  • What are the tokenomics? Is the supply fixed or inflationary?
